All these titles - with limited exceptions are widely available. This list is not up for debate or suggestion. It is simply a guide that covers solely the NineNeins mission as well as a few different options/alternatives - nothing more and nothing less.

I attempted to make it as simple as I could to direct link titles to their hard-copy versions for reference or purchase. Some Titles will require crypto currency - in which for some titles : Ebay is available for the easiest and fastest ways for available copies and/or priceing - in an attempt to avoid the likes of Barnes-Noble, Amazon, etc : By using other mainstream platforms as seldom as possible such as ThriftBooks.

Most non-corporate book shops will require Crypto in which is a double edge sword. Crypto can be a great strength or a great weakness.
