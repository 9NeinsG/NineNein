Default-Paid

If Default Plug-ins are so Wonderful why use anything other or let alone pay for them?

The thing with Audio Plug-ins is they are tools. Each brand has a version of that tool - some are better than others being R&D and Modeled. Others simply re-imagine the concept, some are Dedicated to Purity, others on Character, some on Color.

An example would be SSL and VC.

These are the core of Music Fidelity. Yet, each sounds a bit different. Just as if you bought hardware and you compare your hardware with a friends with the same exact hardware. You would get 2 different sounds extremely subtle but different.
Over time those differences will grow - one will be kept in perfect condition - others will have gone through a much more vigorous life cycle creating all sorts of new hums, slight buzzes, weak knobs, calibrations, etc.

In Audio those tiny little changes end up being MASSIVE differences where the beat up version adds a whole new flavor. Where 4 became 6, and 18db became 10. These little tweaks drastically change the equipments sound overall. Does it make it better than the other? Sometimes.

Myth/Legends

So when a Plug-in gets modeled, its source is Modeled from Particular Boards/HardWare.
Such as in Waves there is SSL Package with 2 versions(G Version and E Version) one has a brown knob one doesn't, in NI there is another version of the SSL which is more modeled after Factory SSL with the modeling and technology of way back when(Another big difference is Era), then recently updated to AAX for use in Protools, in my collection I also own the Official SSL version itself this is what comes in their consoles today - no surprise has the best SSL feel - to a degree ; but thats an entire different conversation. Not always better.
Essentially you are simply buying the same 3 plug-ins(Eq,Dynamic,Buss) multiple times each slightly different.

Such as the V-Series are practically(varies) the same 3 compressors the 2A, 76, and 106. Each version of each one are wildly quite different.

Does the sticky button really make things sound better or is it hype? Is there an all button pushed in hack creating mysteriously awesome sound or absolute rubbish? Can you really hear the sound, changes, differences between an API and SSL? Is this really the best because MJ or some superstar used this for your favorite album?

Music is subjective - although all these can be tested to find out the answers and yes all can be verified True, but they are all also Myths - thats what makes them Legendary. Just because you get the equipment Marilyn Manson has and know all his tips, settings, etc - does not mean you will ever create, sound, or write anything Marilyn Manson does because you don't have him.

Variations

To paint a vivid image of what I am trying to say is if you own a Nintendo Switch, a Bike, or anything you know your gear ; the look, sounds, and feel, etc. Then you go to your friends house and he also has a Nintendo Switch, same Bike, or whatever but his Nintendo Switch is different the controller does not snap off - but his bike has pegs which yours does not, etc. etc.

Same exact plug-ins, all different versions, all change capability's, look, and work-flow.

All in All - The Pro is there are so many options from numerous brands(this creates other compad issues, financial, and system conflicts) and versions creating one endless rabbit hole to explore. The Con is it is a Rabbit Hole that will not make music or create anything at all.
Hence, everything I cover in Plug-ins is a very small sample of what is available even in just Waves alone(There are many fantastic Brands as their is a parallax universe of Plug-ins of a Universal Audio system that requires a financial commitment) - but the List Displayed on Site is an extremely powerful bundle of everything you will ever really need with endless possibilities.

Plug-ins are not Hardware

A Plug-in is an emulation and although very powerful and useful they are not Hardware. For evidence of the starch difference is price.

The SSL Infinite Console(8-64 Single Channels) Plug-in for example can be purchased for $100(NI), $100(Waves), $500(SSL).
An actual SINGLE CHANNEL of a Console(8-64 Single Channels) of the hardware starts at $3,500(500 Series).

For perspective the Console starts at around $25,000.

Compare that to the price of the Plug-in which grants the entire Console of Infinite Channels for only $100-$500. Silly to think that an Unlimited SSL Console ; at half the cost of a Single channel SSLEQ(does not include dyn/buss) you will accomplish SSL quality.

This does not include any of the Rooms, Compressors, or Gear.
Input of Audio is an entirely another aspect of Audio, a Plug-in is an invaluable tool none the less but it is not a replacement of Hardware.

If you could and this was somehow possible there would be no need for Multi-Million dollar studios. Plug-ins simply can-not replace hardware. The world of Analogue is not bounded to 1 and 0 - is very different than Digital. This is not to take from the utility and amazing capabilities and awe of Plug-ins obviously I am a HUGE FAN. But, to put in perspective the reality of Audio innovation.

Plug-ins are by themselves just equipment, tools, and instruments themselves.

Some Plug-ins I mention will seem expensive as will transpire into Instruments - but in the big picture nothing displayed here is expensive when the Big Picture is considered.

Another example is I feature 8 Pianos ranging From Grands, Baby Grands, to Uprights you know basic only a small price tag of $5k-$30k each x 8 - Yet, you can purchase, play, and OWN ALL these EXPENSIVE pianos for only $200. The power of and amazing respect to Sampling.

Will they replace the Real thing... NO! Will they produce and make amazing baby grand cinematic breathtaking music... Yes! Will you be able to tell the difference? Depends.

Important to keep in mind what you are truly getting in a Plug-in - High Quality Audio Creation Production Tools at an extremely low cost.

Pretty sure that if you were to go crazy and buy ALL of Waves, NI, FabFiler, and for Good Measure SSL... it still be 25% the cost of just one of those Pianos, prob 1 of any original Hardware(Compressor) featured/modeled, and maybe working on your 3rd Channel Strip for your SSL Console.

The power of Plug-ins as CLEAR AS DAY.

Plug-in Shine

The sound, quality, and capabilities of Emulations/Plug-ins will all depend on the quality of Audio you Input, if you put in low quality audio, Plug-ins - nor Hardware will help you. The Chain of Import of Audio or the Quality of Audio sent to you is Crucial in the performance of Plug-ins/HardWare.

This is the reason the SSL version of the SSL is not always better at 6x the cost of Waves/NI for it rolls the top and bottom in a curving beautifully way adding Body. The NI Version adds a Mid Warmth normally for tracks lacking Mid-Range I use alot on Transitions. As the Waves Version is neat as it adds a top and sort of thin/tinny element which shines on ridiculously compressed mp3 of most newbies to thin it out.

All helpful but these Corrective aspects is not what these Plugins were designed to do.

The better the source Input Audio the better ALL Plug-ins work. Same as with the Hardware. You would not use a $10 mic through a Plugin 3x that or a Vocal Chain worth say 500-1k dollars - thats like putting Syrup on a Potato and expecting a Lays Potato Chip. What you will get is a $10 mic Audio and a syrup covered Potato.

Every element of Audio gear is dependent on the next Element of Audio gear. Input Audio aka Recording(Studio) is to Capture High Quality Audio. If you are working with High Quality Audio then Plug-ins can be just as good as the Hardware as it moves towards the Mixing(Engineer) but if your source audio is Low Quality then Plug-ins/Hardware will not help you and normally rejected by the Mixer and sent back to the Recording Stage (Studio) to be redone/recaptured - nothing will help except to work on getting Higher Quality Audio.
