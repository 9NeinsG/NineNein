A slew of Downloaders are out there with each one with their strengths, as each one with weaknesses. Any downloader will get the job done but always keep in mind it is just a downloader and they range from Free Open Source to Perpetual Proprietary. In this case I happen to use Wondershares Uniconvert Perpetual Proprietary. A completely overkill downloader.

Granted, it is still my favorite due to being so Feature Rich although I dont use 95% of the program, yet everything I do use it for can be done with any downloader. As the name suggest it is also a converter.
